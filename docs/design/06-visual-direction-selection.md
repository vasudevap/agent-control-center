# Purpose

This document records the evaluation and selection of the visual direction for Atlas.

It captures the rationale behind the chosen direction so future design decisions remain consistent.

---

# Decision

**Selected Direction**

**Modern Infrastructure**

**Status:** Approved as the foundation for the Atlas visual identity.

---

# Design Goal

Create a product that feels like a modern enterprise platform used daily by architects, engineering leaders, consultants, and AI operators.

The interface should communicate confidence through clarity rather than visual spectacle.

---

# Evaluation Summary

## Selected

### Modern Infrastructure

**Outcome:** Selected

**Reasons**

- Best aligns with Atlas positioning as an Agent Control Center.
- Balances enterprise professionalism with contemporary design.
- Scales naturally from individual operators to organizations.
- Supports dense operational interfaces without feeling overwhelming.
- Provides a strong foundation for light and dark themes.

---

## Considered

### Mission Control

Strong operational identity but visually denser than desired.

### Precision Grid

Excellent typography and structure but lacked warmth and approachability.

### Executive Command

Strong executive aesthetic but less suitable for everyday operational workflows.

---

# Design Characteristics

The chosen direction emphasizes:

- Neutral surfaces
- Semantic color usage
- Strong typography
- Generous spacing
- Information-rich layouts
- Minimal visual noise
- Subtle depth
- Structured grids

---
